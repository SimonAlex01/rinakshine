About

What is RINAK SHINE PRAYER CENTER

RINAK SHINE PRAYER CENTER is a registered, non-Government, non-denominational, non-political and non-racial organization HELPING GOD’S PEOPLE HOLISTICALLY.

Background to the Establishment

Before and during Christ ministry, these were a lot of segregations especially between the Jews and none Jews. Even with the coming of Jesus his close confidants thought that salvation was only for Jews however Jesus had come for all.

Even today religious politics have been noticed at a significant level leading to divisions among people of God.

It should be noted that where people are divided even development can never take place.

It is against this background that Rinak Shine Prayer Centre intervenes as Jesus Christ commanded in MARK 16; 15 to preach the Gospel to all mankind.

Most people have had tendency of believing in miracles without their own efforts to overcome poverty and in here, Rinak Shine comes to change that attitude and encourage people to work hard (JAMES 2:14) we do this through sensitization programs and establishment of income generating projects and empowerments with technical /hand on skills especially to the most vulnerable such as widows, youth and orphans.

Achievements

What We Have Achieved

In spite of a number of hindrances we experienced especially during COVID 19 pandemic and the resultant economic challenges, God has been on our side and we have noted the following achievements, so far ever since Rinak Shine Prayer Center became operational i.e.

  • Evangelism

    We have scheduled Saturdays for fellowships in which the word of God is shared, those with demonic affrications prayed for and healed in the name of Jesus Christ, we have made outreaches and people’s souls have been touched.

  • Land Acquisition

    We have been able to acquire land with a 250 seater Hall

  • Water Extension

    We have extended water supply to the premises and installed electricity

  • Procured Seats & Public Address System

    We have procured seats and a public address system

  • Established Agricultural Demonstration Farms

    We have established twelve agricultural demonstration farms majoring in Diary, Rabbitry, Piggery, Poultry, Coffee, and Banana

  • Agricultural Inputs

    We have been able to provide farmers with agricultural inputs comprising of Piglets and Rabbits

  • Established Value Addition Projects

    We have also added value to the following projects: African craft shoes making, books making, yoghurt making, making bathing sponges, and snacks making (donuts, daddies, silverfish)

  • Maggot Breeding and Production

    We have engaged in breeding of Maggots and their production

  • Liquid Fertilizer Production

    We have been able to produce liquid fertilizer

  • Food and Nutrition Project

    We have been able to establish a food and nutrition project which majorly focuses on food fortification processing and packing such as soya bean, silverfish, chia seeds, yellow corn, sorghum, etc.

  • Scholastic Materials Distribution to Children

    We have managed to give scholastic materials to about 73 needy children so far

  • Provided Access to Education

    We also have supported 13 students with school fees for one year as follows: 2 in Secondary schools, 1 in Technical, and 10 in Primary
